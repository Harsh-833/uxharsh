LuxeLuminia

LuxeLuminia is a pioneering skincare brand that offers a comprehensive range of products and free consultations on its website

Overview:

LuxeLuminia is a pioneering skincare brand that offers a comprehensive range of products and free consultations on its website. Our products, designed for various skin types and concerns, blend natural ingredients with scientific formulas. What sets us apart is our personalized skincare consultation service.

Problem Statement

The fear of investing in a product that might not be the perfect fit for our unique skin needs. It's a beauty gamble none of us want to take.

Customers in the skincare market face a high level of uncertainty and risk when choosing products for their individual skin needs.The variety and complexity of skincare products, as well as the lack of reliable and personalized information, make it difficult for customers to find the best products for their skin type, condition, and goals.

Possible Solutions

A website that helps customers find the best skincare products for their individual skin needs and preferences. The website would categorize different products based on their ingredients, benefits, and ratings, and also provide free consultation to the customers through online chat or video call.

Design Process

As the only designer for this project I was responsible for both strategic and visual design.

Key insights derived from interviews

- Primary audience consists of educated, urban females aged 25-34. They seek both product research and purchasing opportunities.

- Users appreciate clear language and find the overall experience satisfactory (average rating of 8.2).

- The personalized consultation service is well-received, with users desiring follow-up emails and live chat support.

- Prioritize product descriptions, reviews, and ingredient transparency for user trust.

- Enhancement of search functionality and ensure mobile responsiveness for a seamless experience.

Key insights derived from the survey

- Users observe skin changes, use tools to track progress, and consider how products make them feel.

- Users use various online and offline sources for skincare information and inspiration. Users also seek expert advice.

- Users select skincare products based on factors like ingredients, benefits, reviews, price, availability, and reputation.

- Users assess skincare productsâ€™ quality, effectiveness, and safety before buying them.

- Users also want evidence-based, transparent, and trustworthy information.

User Persona

With the data collected from the interviews and surveys, I created a persona representing an ideal user of the application. The persona helped me arrive at better solutions as it give an in-depth understanding of the user goals and frustrations and the overall personality.

Empathy Mapping

This process was done to define the target audience with more clarity, as well as illustrate their needs and actions. Empathy mapping helped me gain perspective on their thoughts and feelings. The data in Empathy map are based on insights from user interviews.

User Flow

We created a flow using Miro to illustrate how the user will navigate through the application.

Information Architecture

The card sorting results were further refined to fit the goals of the application. Some of the features were re-categorised to make them easier to find.

Usability Testing

After the visual design was complete I tested the prototype with 5 representative users to see how user friendly the application is. The test was
conducted over Google Meet where the participants were given the following tasks while I observed how they navigated through the application.

  • Sign up into the application
  • Buy a product.
  • Book Free consultation

Suggestions to make the experience better

- Allow users to buy products without creating an account. Some users prefer a quick checkout experience.

- After booking, display a confirmation message and provide next steps (e.g., expect a call within 24 hours).

- Consider adding an option to sign up using social media accounts.
